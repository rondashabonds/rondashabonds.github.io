Info about Drake

Drake is a Canadian singer, songwriter, record producer, and actor. He is known for his unique sound and his ability to create catchy and memorable songs. He has sold over 50 million records worldwide and has won multiple awards, including the Grammy Award for Best New Artist in 2009. Drake is also known for his collaborations with other artists and his ability to create music that is both popular and innovative.

Drake was born on November 13, 1994, in Toronto, Canada. He grew up in a musical family and was exposed to a variety of music genres, including hip hop, R&B, and pop. He began his music career in high school and quickly gained a reputation as a talented songwriter and performer. He released his first album, "Views," in 2012, which became a massive success and helped to establish him as a leading figure in the hip hop and R&B music scenes.
