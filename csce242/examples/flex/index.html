Our Story

Baby Squirrles is a family owned and operated business that has been in business for over 30 years. We are proud to offer our customers a wide variety of high quality pet supplies at an affordable price. Our staff is friendly and knowledgeable, and we are always happy to help you find the perfect pet supplies for your furry friend. We take pride in our work and strive to provide the best possible customer service. Thank you for choosing Baby Squirrles for your pet supplies.
